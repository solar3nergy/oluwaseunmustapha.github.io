RACISM IN POLICING?

This R programming language project aims to identify factors that contribute to racial inequities in policing by examining census and police department deployment data. The project will analyze the data to identify deployment regions where racial disparities exist that are not explained by crime rates or poverty levels. The ultimate goal is to provide law enforcement organizations with insights into areas where they can improve by addressing racial disparities. By using data-driven approaches, this project aims to inform policy and decision-making to promote more equitable policing practices.
